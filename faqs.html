Question 1:How we can charge thermopacks?

Thermopacks can be cahrged using refrigerator, freezer or container of ice water.Thermopacks must be charge unitl liquid phase changes to solid.

Question 2:What is included with the Cooling Jacket?

Cooling jackets come with set of thermopacks and instruction for product care and use. Spare Thermopacks are not included, but can be purchased through Order.

Question 3:How many thermpacks are there in each Cooling Jacket?

Each Cooling Jacket consist of 7 cooling packs.4 in front side and 3 in back sides.

Question 4:How many different sizes of cooling jackets are there?

Question 5:How often do the thermopacks need to be replaced?

Material inside a Thermopacks never stop working.It can be used for indefinitely period of time until thermopacks do not get damage.
